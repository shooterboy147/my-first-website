My Crypto Journey & Lessons

I started hearing about Bitcoin back in 2015–2016 when I was just 11 or 12. I didn’t take it seriously then — I was focused on playing football and just being a kid.

It wasn’t until around 2019, when I was finishing secondary school, that I started hearing crypto-related terms more often. I became more familiar, but I still didn’t act on it.

In 2023, I began following some crypto accounts on Twitter. I didn’t understand them — just charts and numbers. It was overwhelming. Then in early 2024, I came across a tweet from Shola offering free crypto education through his Telegram group CryptoVishola.

That group changed everything. I learned how to trade, how to use signals, and how to read charts. I wasn’t perfect — I was still busy and didn’t practice enough. Eventually, I got liquidated.

My biggest lesson: Don’t trade with emotions. After losing money, I panicked and tried to recover it instantly — and lost even more. I learned to be patient, to think clearly, and to analyze before jumping back in.

I created Bitcoin Secure to help others like me — people who are hungry to learn, grow, and win. I want to build a platform that motivates people, connects like-minds, and shows we can win legally through knowledge and smart strategy.

This is for anyone out there chasing a dream. You’re not alone.
